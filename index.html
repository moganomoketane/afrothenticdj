WHO IS

Origins

Born in Limpopo,
built in Joburg.

From his early days discovering a passion for music in Ga-Molepo, Limpopo, DJ Afrothentic's journey is one of authenticity and relentless drive — a path that led him across Johannesburg and Pretoria's most sought-after venues and events. A graduate of the University of Johannesburg with a Diploma and Advanced Diploma in Business Information Technology, he formalised his craft at Rise Academy in 2021, forging a sound that bridges deep African roots with the precision of modern Afrohouse and Afrotech.

That same passion for the culture led him to give back as a former DJ Mentor at Rise Academy, Rosebank — shaping the next wave of South African DJ talent before stepping back to focus fully on his own artistry.

Technical Rider

DJ Afrothentic — Sound & Equipment Requirements

Download PDF

DJ Equipment

  • Pioneer CDJ 2000 Nexus / CDJ 3000
  • Pioneer DJM 900 Nexus / DJM A9 Mixer
  • Pioneer Opus Quad
  • Pioneer CDJ 1000 / 2000 / 3000 series
  • Pioneer or Denon professional controllers
  • Industry-standard USB compatible setup

CDJ 350 is NOT acceptable. Equipment must be fully functional and set up before performance time.

Sound System

  • Professional, high-quality system suitable for venue capacity
  • Strong, clean bass response — essential for Afrohouse & Afrotech
  • Stable and secure DJ booth/table
  • Fully functional booth monitors

Performance Terms

  • Performance time to be agreed in advance
  • Full payment terms to be agreed prior to event date
  • Any recording, live streaming, or media usage must be communicated beforehand
